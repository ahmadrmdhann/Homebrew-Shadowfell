# Changelog - Fall of the Shadowfell

All notable changes to the Fall of the Shadowfell homebrew D&D content will be documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.0.0/),
and this project adheres to [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/spec/v2.0.0.html).

## [1.3.1] - 2024-05-24

### Added
#### Sorcerer Subclasses
- **Shadowborn Sorcerer**
  - Origin features at levels 3, 6, 14, and 18
  - Born of Shadows: Enhanced darkvision and shadow detection
  - Constitution of the Dark: Shadow Walk teleportation and Strength of Death feature
  - Hounds of Omen: Summon Shadow Mastiffs to hunt enemies
  - Umbral Form: Transform into shadowy form with damage immunity
  
- **Shaded Soul Sorcerer**
  - Origin features at levels 3, 6, 14, and 18
  - A Shadow Beyond Sight: Invisibility on spell hit/save
  - Eyes of the Dark: Enhanced darkvision and darkness spell
  - Cloak of Enveloping Shade: Protective shadow cloak with AC bonus
  - Eclipse Dome: Create zone of magical darkness affecting enemies
  - Umbral Wave: Blind enemies with shadow magic

#### Optional Variant Class Features
- **Heart of the Eclipse**: Shadow magic eruption on spell casting
- **Shade Form**: Reaction to turn into shadow and avoid attacks
- **Weapon of Shade**: Summon magical shadow weapons with extra attack
- **Grasp of the Eclipse**: Shadow-infused arm touch with devastating effects
- **Nevermore's Wings**: Shadow raven wings for flight and damage
- **Umbral Mist**: Area denial with shadow mist and teleportation

#### Items
- **Miner's Tools**: New tool for mining and excavation operations

#### Backgrounds
- **Shadow Miner**: Deep underground workers touched by Shadowfell
- **Corrupted Bureaucrat**: Former officials who know state secrets
- **Iron Law Enforcer**: Disillusioned law enforcement officers
- **Shadowfell Researcher**: Academics studying shadow magic
- **Street Revolutionary**: Urban resistance fighters
- **Crime Syndicate Operative**: Underground criminal operatives
- **Exiled Noble**: Fallen aristocrats seeking redemption
- **Cloaked Informant**: Master spies and information brokers
- **Fallen Knight**: Oathbreakers seeking new purpose
- **Shadow-Touched Orphan**: Children raised in shadow-tainted areas

#### Feats
- **Deep Delver**: Enhanced underground survival and tremorsense
- **Forger's Insight**: Document forgery and bureaucratic deception
- **Badge of Authority**: Command spell and intimidation advantages
- **Shadow Scholar**: Detect shadow magic and necromancy cantrip
- **Urban Guerrilla**: Street movement and defiant spirit
- **Black Market Contacts**: Thieves' cant and underworld dealings
- **Noble Presence**: Heroism spell and social influence points
- **Face Dancer**: Disguise self spell and insight disadvantage
- **Oathbreaker's Strike**: Heavy armor proficiency and necrotic strikes
- **Child of the Gloom**: Necrotic resistance and shadow thaumaturgy

#### Spells
##### Cantrips (0th Level)
- **Grasp of Shadows**: Touch attack with healing prevention
- **Shade Bolt**: Ranged shadow projectile
- **Shadow Burst**: Area burst around caster
- **Ray of Shade**: Beam that reduces maximum health

##### 1st Level Spells
- **Darts of Shade**: Multiple shadow darts with scaling
- **Grasping Shadow Bolt**: Sustained shadow beam

##### 2nd Level Spells
- **Pillar of Ill Omen**: Cylinder of darkness damage
- **Shadow Binds**: Movement restraint with shadow strips

##### 3rd Level Spells
- **Umbral Step**: Teleportation with area damage
- **Umbral Orb**: Large area shadow explosion

##### 4th Level Spells
- **Blade of the Raven Queen's Servant**: Conjure magical scythe

##### 5th Level Spells
- **Aura of Shade**: Self-centered damage aura

##### 6th Level Spells
- **Investiture of Darkness**: Shadow-based investiture variant

##### 7th Level Spells
- **Decay**: Devastating touch spell with ongoing damage

##### 8th Level Spells
- **Dread Spear**: Massive line attack with lasting darkness

##### 9th Level Spells
- **World of Darkness**: Ultimate shadow magic with massive consequences
